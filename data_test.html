We appreciate your continued trust in Hamilton Energy. Once again, it is our pleasure to provide you with a selection of Hamilton Energy Advantage Plans that can help you save a considerable amount of money on the cost of propane. For your convenience, we have five different plans to choose from. Each is exclusive to Hamilton Energy and enables you to choose the plan that best fits your situation.

Our savings plans are listed on the enrollment form in order of their popularity. To be eligible, a tank size of at least 250 gallons is required. If you have questions about our Advantage Plans, or need assistance deciding which is best for you, simply call 1-888-GAS-2YOU (1-888-427-2968).

  1. If you enrolled in a plan last year and wish to keep that plan, simply pay the invoice that you received in the mail. Your previous year plan will be indicated on the invoice you receive.
  2. If you are new Advantage Plan customer or wish to select a different Plan from the previous year, please return the enrollment form that you received in the mail, make your selection and return it, with your enrollment fee.
  3. If you choose Plan 4 (Pre-Buy), please fill out the enrollment form and return it, with your calculated amount.
